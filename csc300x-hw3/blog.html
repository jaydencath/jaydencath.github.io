SUSHI REPUBLIC

Location: 329 Tate St, Greensboro, NC

Sushi Republic is a locally-owned Japanese-inspired restaurant that was voted Best Sushi in Greensboro by Readers for Triad's Best on Yes Weekly! They currently do not have any specials but they specialize in classic Japanese cuisine as well as diverse fusion dishes from a vast amount of different cultures.

CHIPOTLE

Location: 435 Tate St, Greensboro, NC

Although this is a fast-food chain, Chipotle is renowned as one of the most prominent and well respected fast-food establishments. They're a Mexican fast-food restaurant that specializes in burritos, tacos, & bowls. Their most recent special was their take on Carne Asada.

EAST COAST WINGS

Location: 338 Tate St, Greensboro, NC

East Coast Wings is a locally-owned franchisee that has won Best Hot Sauce at the National Buffalo Wing Festival in Buffalo, NY that specializes in burgers, flatbreads, skillets, wings, and craft beer.
